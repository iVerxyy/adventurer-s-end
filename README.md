## 🧠 About the Game

**Adventurer's End** is a strategic puzzle game where every move matters.

Navigate treacherous dungeons filled with mysterious black holes that pull you closer every 2 seconds. Push boxes onto goals while managing your precious rewind energy — because in this adventure, mistakes cost more than time.

You play as the **blue orb 🔵**, solving increasingly complex puzzles while fighting against time and gravity.

## ✨ Features

- ✧ **12 Handcrafted Levels**  
  From beginner-friendly to brain-melting challenges  

- ✧ **Time Rewind Mechanic**  
  Hold **R** or click to reverse your mistakes — but watch your energy  

- ✧ **Dynamic Black Holes**  
  They pull you in every 2 seconds. Plan ahead or get consumed  

- ✧ **Energy Management System**  
  Crystals restore your rewind power, but they don’t regenerate  

- ✧ **Achievement System**  
  Unlock 6 achievements through speed, efficiency, and skill  

- ✧ **Level Editor** *(unlocks at Level 6)*  
  Create your own puzzles  

- ✧ **Progressive Unlocking**  
  Earn your way through each challenge
